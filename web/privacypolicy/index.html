Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 26, 2025

1. Overview

Bubble Contact Web is a fully offline desktop application developed by Marble Minds Studio. This privacy policy outlines how we handle user data and protect your privacy.

2. Data Collection and Use

We do not collect any user or device data for analytics, marketing, or advertising. Contact information entered by the user is stored locally on your device only.

3. Data Sharing

No data is transmitted or shared externally. All information remains on the user’s device and is accessible only to that device's users.

4. Permissions

The app currently does not request any sensitive permissions. Future versions may include optional access to system contacts for import/export, with proper user consent.

5. Children’s Privacy

This application is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Ads

Bubble Contact Web does not display advertisements or use ad-related SDKs.

7. App Access

All app features are currently available without login or membership. Future updates may introduce contact limits and paid upgrades.
